Future Work

In this project, Random Forest and LSTM architectures were implemented to predict flight delays. The Random Forest model produced strong performance for binary classification, while the LSTM model achieved higher training accuracy because of its ability to process sequential data effectively. However, overfitting remained a challenge in the LSTM model. Future improvements will focus on increasing the dataset size, integrating additional parameters such as airport traffic flow, weather visibility, and designing more advanced deep learning architectures to improve prediction accuracy and system reliability.
